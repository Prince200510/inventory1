Documentation

Step 1: Register

Create an account by providing necessary information such as name, username, email, and password.

Step 2: Sign In

After registering, sign in with your credentials to access the system.

Note: Make sure you have copied a passcode which can be used in future is you forgeted a password.

Step 3: Dashboard

Upon successful login, you will be directed to the dashboard where you can manage your inventory and perform various tasks related to your business.
